# Instructional Materials Curriculum Overlay

## Purpose

Bridge overlay for curriculum-facing instructional-materials review when the task is vocabulary planning rather than Drive artifact generation.

## Inherited Standards

See `../_common-overlay-rules.md` plus:

- `01_Shared_Standards/curriculum/curriculum-vocabulary-planner-response-standard.md`
- `01_Shared_Standards/instructional-design/student-language-standard.md`

## Vocabulary Review Rule

When lesson vocabulary affects slides, worksheets, guided notes, rubrics, or student directions, use the Curriculum Vocabulary Planner Response Standard before drafting or revising student-facing language.

## Stop Conditions

Stop and route to `02_Agent_Overlays/instructional-materials-coach.md` when the request requires generating, editing, copying, or publishing classroom materials.
